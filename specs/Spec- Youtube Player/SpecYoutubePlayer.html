Introduction

YoutubePlayer is a wrapper around the Youtube embedded player API, documented at https://developers.google.com/youtube/js_api_reference and https://developers.google.com/youtube/iframe_api_reference . If you have opted into the YouTube HTML5 beta at http://www.youtube.com/html5 then you will get HTML5 video, otherwise you will get Flash video

Properties

autoplay

Boolean. Set to true to automatically load and play videos set with playlist or videoId

videoId

String. The ID of a Youtube video to play. Clears the playlist

playlist

Array | String. An array of videoIds or a single playlistId

width

height

Methods

play()

Start playing the video

pause()

Pause the video

stop()

Stop playing, and stop loading any more of the video.
